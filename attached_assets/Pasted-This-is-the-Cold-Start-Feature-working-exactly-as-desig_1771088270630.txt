The Reason for Silence
In your code (google-alerts-monitor.ts), there is a specific safety mechanism:
> "On the very first scan, do not alert. Just mark all existing posts as 'Seen'."
> 
Why? If you added a client and the bot immediately sent you the last 50 posts from RSS, your phone would explode. It is designed to only tell you about NEW things that happen after you hit start.
