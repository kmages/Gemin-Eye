You want **noise**. You want the bot to feel like a stock exchange floor.

To do that, we need a client that targets a topic with **insane global volume**, high emotion, and constant "new" posts.

Here is the **"Nuclear Option"** for testing:

### **The Client: "CoinSniper AI"**

* **The Business:** An AI tool that finds "100x Gem" cryptocurrencies before they pump.
* **The Vibe:** High energy, FOMO, "Degen" culture.
* **Why it works:** Crypto never sleeps. There is a new post every 4 seconds asking "What should I buy?" or "Is this a scam?"

---

### **How to Spawn the Chaos (Copy & Paste):**

**Step 1: Create the Client**
Send this to your Telegram Bot:
`/newclient`

**Step 2: The Setup (Paste these answers)**

* **Name:** `CoinSniper AI`
* **Type:** `Crypto Analytics Tool`
* **Audience:** `Crypto traders looking for high-risk, high-reward memecoins`
* **Offering:** `Real-time alerts for undervalued tokens on Solana and Base chains.`
* **Tone:** `1` (Casual/Hype)
* **Keywords:** `100x, gem, moon, solana, memecoin, pump, alpha, token, presale, wagmi`

**Step 3: Connect the "Firehose" (RSS Feeds)**
Once the client is made, add these **high-volume Reddit feeds** one by one.

Send: `/addalert` -> Select **CoinSniper AI** -> Paste URL:

1. `https://www.reddit.com/r/CryptoMoonShots/new.rss` (The spammiest place on earth)
2. `https://www.reddit.com/r/Solana/new.rss` (Constant questions)
3. `https://www.reddit.com/r/SatoshiStreetBets/new.rss` (High energy)
